About Project:

In this project, Tableau is used to analyze the Netflix dataset from 2008 to 2020.

The project includes various types of visualizations such as:

  • Geographical charts
  • Bar charts
  • Pie charts
  • Line charts
  • Interactive dashboards

The dataset consists of extensive information about movies and TV shows available on Netflix during this period.

Using Tableau, the data is visualized to explore trends, genres, viewership patterns, and more.

There is a selection feature where users can choose a movie or TV show to view detailed descriptions, ratings, and related insights.

The goal of this project is to provide insights into Netflix content trends over the years and enhance understanding through interactive data visualizations.
